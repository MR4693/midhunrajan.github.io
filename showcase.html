Manufacturing Problem

Built to solve a real production problem. Not a theoretical one.

Before: Paper-Dependent Production

How Ludwick Precision managed jobs before this platform

  • Paper job packs printed daily by supervisors and distributed manually
  • Operators wrote start/end times on physical job cards — transcribed to ERP at end of shift, often inaccurately
  • No visibility into who was working on which job, or for how long
  • Move tickets handwritten — frequently lost between work centres
  • Job queue only updated once per shift; operators couldn't see real-time priorities
  • Production drawings stored in folders — operators requested copies from supervisors
  • No digital audit trail of what was produced, when, or by whom

After: Live Digital Operations

What the Manufacturing Operations Platform replaced this with

  • Android tablets at every work centre — operators log in with a kiosk number
  • Clock In / Out writes production records to MIE Trak in real time
  • Full operator attribution on every work order — timestamped to the second
  • Move tickets generated automatically on job completion, with Brother label printer integration
  • Live job queue pulled from ERP on every screen load — always current
  • Production drawings and job documentation accessible directly on screen
  • Complete digital audit trail — visible to management via reporting dashboard

sc-06-clockout.jpg
Clock Out Modal
Cancel — no record written

Operator didn't proceed. No production record created. Job remains in queue unchanged. Clock-in time discarded.

Incomplete — partial progress

Records partial qty. ERP updated with progress. Job stays active. Remaining quantity visible on next operator's session.

Complete → Move Ticket triggered

Sets MIE Trak operation status to Complete. Triggers automatic Move Ticket generation. No supervisor action needed.

Business Value

Production actuals — labour hours, quantity, operator — written to MIE Trak on every clock out. Management dashboards update in real time.

ERP Integration

POST to MIE Trak Labour API with completed quantity and outcome. WO operation status updated. Routing progression triggered on Complete.

Three Outcomes

Cancel, Incomplete, and Complete each trigger different ERP writes — ensuring the system always reflects the true state of production.

Manufacturing Problem

Previously: end-of-shift manual batch entry. Quantity completed often estimated. Labour hours rounded. Inaccurate production data across the board.
